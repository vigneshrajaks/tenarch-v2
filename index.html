Product Overview

Sifting through resumes is time-consuming and can be full of noise. Tenarch does the pre-work
of reviewing resumes, recruiting phone screens, and getting references, so you can spend your
time on the higher value aspects of hiring. Tenarch gathers all the right research-backed signals you need to confidently decide to interview talent, and surfaces them in our innovative Tenarch Talent Profile.

The Tenarch Talent Profile is a dashboard that highlights areas of cultural and skill alignment between your team and the talent, and areas for discussion. The profile includes tips on how to
conduct the most effective interview for each particular candidate based on your company’s
specific needs.

Key Benefits

  • Quickly identify the right talent for the job, to fit both the role and your organization

  • Avoid bias in your hiring process, so you don’t miss out on the best people

  • Conduct the most effective interview, saving your time and creating and engaging candidate experience

How do we match you with the right talent?

Work Styles Fit

How well a person and their team are a mutual fit directly affects employee performance, retention, and overall success, so let Tenarch help you effectively match each candidate's preferences with yours for the best fit. View the candidate’s potential “culture fit”, or Work Styles fit, at a glance and immediately identify both areas of strong alignment, and areas of possible divergence.

Match with candidates on eight research-backed factors that impact fit, like Integrity, Collaboration, Adaptability, Results-orientation, Detail-orientation, Customer-orientation, Rigorousness, and Competitiveness. Tenarch highlights areas of strong alignment, and possible misalignment, so you can focus your interview time on the things that matter most.

Experience Fit

Tenarch uses machine learning algorithms to pick up relevant signals from a candidate’s profile and highlight the most important ones so you can quickly gain confidence in their skill set without parsing through a wall of text.

We combine this with their detailed work experience, giving you a complete overview of their work history, highlighting significant accomplishments. Tenarch also provides relevant insights for you to easily reference during your interviews.

Talent Fit

Tenarch’s canddiate intake process provides a clear signal for what is most important to the talent in their next role. This helps you feel confident in the match, and more critically helps you articulate the opportunity so they feel confident in the match, too.
